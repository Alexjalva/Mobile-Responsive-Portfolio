About Me

I am a baller. I am a shot caller. People sometimes ask me how I manage to be both, and although I don't always have an answer that I can explain in words, I just show them my portfolio and they begin to understand.

This website was created as a magnum opus of sorts - an example of the greatness of man, if you will. You may find yourself wondering "How did I end up here?" or "Why can't I leave?". The truth is, there is no real answer to either of these questions, besides the answer that you have to find yourself. Admiration.

It's not jealousy. It's just being in awe at something so great. It's natural and nobody can blame you.

My name is Alex Alvarado as you may have guessed from the name at the top of the page or just from taking a shot in the dark. It's a common name, but don't let that fool you. Everyone else that has this name is only borrowing, my usage is the one and only true application of the name. I work in marketing for biotechnology, and despite knowing how marketing strategies try to reach me, they end up convincing me to buy something every time. I am weak, but only in weakness is there strength.

Now that you know about me, it is only fair that I know about you. Write a letter describing yourself and drop it in your nearest mailbox. There is a non-zero chance that it reaches me, and then we will both know about each other.

Regards,
Alex
